About Us

Welcome to MR-International Limited One of Nigerian’s foremost ICT Company.

Since our entry into Nigeria’s ICT industry we have developed our MRsoft brand to be synonymous with excellence and customer satisfaction.

We provide our clients with innovative and customized solutions to promote the effectiveness and efficiency of their enterprises.

Our products and services are backed by a bouquet of bespoke training programmes and a 24hours support team. MRsoft is the registered trademark of M-R International Nigeria limited which is an indigenous first class information and communication technology company.

M-R International was established in 1992 and registered by Nigeria’s Corporate Affairs Commission to provide ICT solutions, offer specialized trainings to equip individuals with ICT skills to promote entrepreneurial activities and to offer consultancy services to individuals and corporate organisations who transact business with and in the ICT industry.

Operating from our corporate head office, MRsoft Technology Complex, Port Harcourt, Nigeria, M-R International has metamorphosed into an industry brand synonymous with quality and professionalism in the delivery of ICT solutions.
